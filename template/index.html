JPL-CIT Pulsar Timing group

We are the NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) and Caltech gravitational wave and pulsar timing research group. Our research specializations and interests include gravitational wave detection astrophysics, data analysis, pulsar timing (array) science, and most things related to these topics. A major drive for all our research is the desire to do astrophysics with gravitational-wave observations. Especially now that the Laser Interferometer Gravitational Observatory (LIGO) has detected gravitational waves, we believe that this is truly where the exciting science is happening right now. Our place in the gravitational-wave scientific community is as a part of the North American Nanohertz Observatory for Gravitational waves (NANOGrav), through which our group has been heavily involved in the various gravitational-wave searches in NANOGrav pulsar timing data.
